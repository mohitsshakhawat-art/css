Cherry Blossoms:
The Symbolism and Beauty of Sakura

the delicate pink or white flowers of ornamental cherry trees that symbolize spring, renewal, and the transient beauty of life. They are deeply significant in Japanese culture, celebrated annually through the tradition of hanami (flower viewing), which involves gatherings under the blooming trees. Originating in East Asia, these trees have been cultivated and distributed globally, with festivals now held in many parts of the world
